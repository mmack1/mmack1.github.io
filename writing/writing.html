Current and Future Projects

AI-Powered Accessibility Tool: I'm working on improving a screen reader project that I designed in 2022 for visually impaired individuals and people with learning or neurodevelopmental disabilities. I want to improve and application navigation by integrating machine learning and computer vision. I hope this tool could evolve into an app that leverages voice recognition and text-to-speech technology to make digital spaces more accessible and user-friendly.

Interactive Air Quality Monitor: Inspired by the concept of a Tamagotchi for air quality, I am working on a project explores creating a device that tracks environmental conditions in a room using hardware sensors. By integrating components like the PMS5003 for particulate matter and the BME680 for temperature, humidity, and air quality, the goal is to provide real-time feedback in an engaging, interactive way. Alternatively, I have been considering an app-based version that could analyze local environmental conditions using data from weather APIs like OpenWeatherMap or AirVisual. Both focus on making air quality awareness more accessible and engaging through intuitive design and potential gamification elements.

Right now, it feels a bit disconnected from page to page, and I’d like to create a fully scrollable experience that flows seamlessly. It should be responsive, ensuring it adapts well to different screen sizes, including phones and tablets. Optimizing for speed is a priority, whether that means reducing render-blocking resources, improving data fetching efficiency, or leveraging caching strategies. Additionally, the overall design could be more visually appealing and cohesive. I want to refine the aesthetic to create a polished, modern look that enhances readability and engagement. To improve performance and scalability, I plan to integrate a proxy server like Node.js. This would help with efficient data handling, reduce latency, and provide more flexibility in managing API requests. By making these updates, I aim to create a smooth, high-performing website that feels immersive and intuitive for users.
